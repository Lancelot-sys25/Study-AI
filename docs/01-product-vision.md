# Product Vision: LearnOS AI

## 1. Executive Summary

LearnOS AI la nen tang hoc tap thong minh su dung AI de giup nguoi hoc hoc nhanh hon, ghi nho lau hon va duy tri dong luc hoc tap ben vung. San pham ket hop flashcard thong minh, spaced repetition, adaptive quiz, AI tutor, roadmap ca nhan hoa, planner, analytics va gamification trong mot trai nghiem hoc tap lien mach.

Muc tieu cua LearnOS AI khong chi la tao cong cu hoc tap, ma la xay dung mot he sinh thai co the dong hanh voi nguoi hoc tu luc nhap tai lieu, bien kien thuc thanh noi dung hoc, luyen tap, on tap, do luong tien bo, den khi dat muc tieu cu the nhu thi chung chi, hoc lap trinh, hoc ngoai ngu hoac nang cao ky nang nghe nghiep.

## 2. Product Vision Statement

LearnOS AI tro thanh nen tang hoc tap ca nhan hoa, noi moi nguoi hoc co mot AI tutor rieng co kha nang hieu muc tieu, nang luc, thoi quen, diem yeu va dong luc cua ho, tu do tu dong thiet ke lo trinh hoc, tao bai tap, nhac on tap va giup nguoi hoc khong bo cuoc giua chung.

## 3. Mission

Giup nguoi hoc:

- Hoc dung noi dung can hoc vao dung thoi diem.
- Bien tai lieu dai va kho thanh flashcard, quiz, note va roadmap de hoc duoc ngay.
- Ghi nho lau hon bang spaced repetition ket hop AI.
- Nhan duoc giai thich ca nhan hoa nhu co gia su rieng.
- Duy tri thoi quen hoc bang gamification, goal va reminder thong minh.
- Theo doi tien bo bang analytics ro rang, de hanh dong.

## 4. Target Users

### Student

Nguoi hoc la nhom nguoi dung chinh. Ho can mot cong cu giup quan ly tai lieu, tao flashcard, luyen quiz, nhan giai thich va theo doi tien bo.

Nhu cau chinh:

- Hoc cho bai kiem tra, thi chung chi, mon hoc o truong.
- Can lo trinh ro rang khi hoc mot ky nang moi.
- Can AI giai thich de hieu bai nhanh hon.
- Can he thong nhac on tap va tao dong luc.

### Teacher

Giao vien can cong cu tao hoc lieu nhanh, theo doi tien bo lop hoc va phat hien hoc sinh can ho tro.

Nhu cau chinh:

- Tao flashcard, quiz, challenge tu giao trinh.
- Giao bai va theo doi tien do.
- Xem hoc sinh yeu, hoc sinh noi bat.
- Gui feedback, comment va tai lieu bo sung.

### Parent

Phu huynh can xem tinh hinh hoc tap cua con mot cach de hieu, khong qua ky thuat.

Nhu cau chinh:

- Xem thoi gian hoc, muc do hoan thanh, streak.
- Nhan bao cao hang tuan.
- Biet con dang manh/yếu o dau.
- Nhan goi y ho tro phu hop.

### Admin

Quan tri vien can quan ly nguoi dung, noi dung, he thong, bao cao va van hanh.

Nhu cau chinh:

- Quan ly tai khoan, role, permission.
- Theo doi DAU, MAU, storage, AI usage.
- Giam sat noi dung, report va audit log.
- Cau hinh goi dich vu, thanh toan, policy.

## 5. Core Value Proposition

LearnOS AI khac biet o kha nang ket noi toan bo chu trinh hoc tap:

1. Import tai lieu.
2. AI hieu noi dung va trich xuat kien thuc quan trong.
3. AI tao flashcard, quiz, note, cheat sheet va mindmap.
4. He thong lap lich on tap bang spaced repetition.
5. Quiz thay doi do kho theo nang luc.
6. AI tutor giai thich khi nguoi hoc sai.
7. Analytics chi ra diem yeu va de xuat hanh dong tiep theo.
8. Gamification giup nguoi hoc duy tri thoi quen.

San pham tap trung vao ket qua hoc tap, khong chi tap trung vao viec luu tru noi dung.

## 6. Product Principles

### Personalization First

Moi nguoi hoc co muc tieu, toc do, diem yeu va thoi quen khac nhau. San pham phai uu tien ca nhan hoa roadmap, lich on tap, quiz, reminder va noi dung giai thich.

### Learning Loop Over Feature Count

Moi tinh nang phai phuc vu vong lap hoc tap:

Learn -> Practice -> Review -> Reflect -> Improve.

Neu mot tinh nang khong giup nguoi hoc tien bo, no khong nen la uu tien cao.

### AI as Tutor, Not Decoration

AI khong chi la chat box. AI phai tham gia vao:

- Phan tich tai lieu.
- Tao hoc lieu.
- Giai thich loi sai.
- Dieu chinh do kho.
- De xuat lich hoc.
- Phat hien nguy co bo cuoc.

### Motivation by Design

He thong phai giup nguoi hoc tiep tuc hoc ngay ca khi mat dong luc. Gamification can ho tro thoi quen that, khong bien san pham thanh tro choi vo nghia.

### Trust, Safety, and Privacy

Du lieu hoc tap, tai lieu ca nhan, lich su chat va thong tin tre em co tinh nhay cam. San pham phai duoc thiet ke voi bao mat, quyen rieng tu, audit log va kiem soat truy cap ngay tu dau.

## 7. Business Goals

### Short Term

- Tao MVP co the demo vong lap hoc tap chinh.
- Cho phep nguoi dung import noi dung text/document va tao flashcard, quiz.
- Co dashboard tien bo, AI assistant va spaced repetition co ban.
- Thu thap feedback tu nguoi hoc that.

### Mid Term

- Mo rong sang teacher dashboard va study group.
- Tich hop OpenAI API, embedding va RAG.
- Ho tro nhieu loai document: PDF, Word, PPT, image OCR, YouTube.
- Tao adaptive quiz engine hoan chinh.
- Them gamification, notification va analytics nang cao.

### Long Term

- Tro thanh nen tang hoc tap AI da vai tro cho student, teacher, parent va admin.
- Ho tro marketplace hoc lieu, lop hoc, subscription va enterprise school deployment.
- Xay dung mo hinh recommendation rieng dua tren hanh vi hoc tap.
- Ho tro offline learning va dong bo da thiet bi.

## 8. Product Scope

### MVP Scope

MVP nen tap trung vao mot luong hoc tap hoan chinh:

- Dang ky, dang nhap co ban.
- Student dashboard.
- Tao flashcard tu text/markdown/PDF co ban.
- Flashcard review voi spaced repetition ban dau.
- Quiz co mot so dang co ban: multiple choice, true/false, fill blank.
- AI tutor chat ket noi OpenAI API.
- Roadmap ca nhan hoa don gian.
- Study planner va reminder noi bo.
- Analytics co ban: study time, accuracy, completion, streak.

### Post-MVP Scope

- OAuth Google/Microsoft.
- MFA, OTP, email verification.
- Teacher, parent, admin dashboards.
- Collaboration va study group.
- Advanced quiz modes.
- FSRS va ML-based scheduling.
- RAG tren tai lieu ca nhan.
- Push/email notification.
- Gamification shop, rank, league.
- Offline mode.
- Docker, CI/CD, Azure deployment.

### Out of Scope for MVP

- Voice chat.
- Full video/audio transcription pipeline.
- Marketplace hoc lieu.
- Payment/subscription.
- Mobile native app.
- Custom ML training pipeline rieng.
- Enterprise SSO.

## 9. High-Level Product Modules

### Authentication and User Management

Quan ly login, register, session, role, permission va profile hoc tap.

### Learning Dashboard

Tong hop tinh trang hoc tap: thoi gian hoc, streak, completion, flashcard can on, quiz hom nay, goal, progress va AI recommendation.

### Smart Flashcard

Tao, quan ly, review va on tap flashcard. AI co the sinh question, answer, hint, explanation, difficulty, tag va confidence score.

### Spaced Repetition Engine

Lap lich on tap dua tren ket qua tra loi, do kho, thoi gian phan hoi va lich su quen.

### AI Learning Assistant

AI tutor co kha nang giai thich, tom tat, tao quiz, tao flashcard, tao note, tao example va kiem tra bai lam.

### Smart Quiz Engine

Tao va cham quiz voi nhieu dang cau hoi. Do kho thay doi theo nang luc nguoi hoc.

### Personalization Engine

Phan tich diem manh, diem yeu, phong cach hoc, toc do hoc va muc do hoan thanh de de xuat roadmap, challenge va reminder.

### Roadmap and Planner

Tao lo trinh hoc theo muc tieu, deadline, trinh do va thoi gian ranh.

### Notes and Documents

Quan ly note, document, link va file hoc tap. Ho tro search, tag, folder va AI summary.

### Gamification

XP, coin, badge, streak, rank, league, daily reward va challenge.

### Collaboration

Study group, shared flashcard, shared note, discussion va teacher feedback.

### Analytics and Reporting

Bao cao cho student, teacher, parent va admin.

## 11. Competitive Positioning

### Compared with Quizlet

LearnOS AI khong chi tao va on flashcard, ma ket hop roadmap, AI tutor, analytics, adaptive quiz va document intelligence.

### Compared with Duolingo

LearnOS AI hoc duoc nhieu mon va ky nang khac nhau, khong chi ngon ngu, dong thoi cho phep nguoi dung import tai lieu rieng.

### Compared with Notion AI

LearnOS AI khong chi viet va tom tat note, ma bien note thanh lich hoc, flashcard, quiz va bao cao tien bo.

### Compared with Coursera/Khan Academy

LearnOS AI ca nhan hoa dua tren tai lieu va muc tieu rieng cua nguoi hoc, khong chi dua tren course co san.

## 12. Key Risks

### AI Accuracy Risk

AI co the tao noi dung sai hoac giai thich thieu chinh xac.

Mitigation:

- Hien thi AI confidence score.
- Cho phep nguoi dung report/sua flashcard.
- Dung RAG voi citation tu tai lieu nguon.
- Tao review flow cho teacher.

### User Motivation Risk

Nguoi hoc co the bo cuoc sau vai ngay.

Mitigation:

- Muc tieu nho moi ngay.
- Adaptive difficulty.
- Reminder ca nhan hoa.
- Streak, reward va challenge vua suc.

### Product Complexity Risk

Prompt co pham vi rat lon, de dan den san pham bi dan trai.

Mitigation:

- MVP tap trung vao learning loop chinh.
- Tach module theo roadmap.
- Uu tien feature theo impact va effort.

### Cost Risk

AI, embedding va storage co the ton kem khi scale.

Mitigation:

- Cache ket qua AI.
- Batch processing.
- Gioi han quota theo plan.
- Theo doi AI usage per user.

### Privacy Risk

Nguoi dung upload tai lieu rieng va du lieu hoc tap nhay cam.

Mitigation:

- Encryption at rest va in transit.
- RBAC.
- Audit log.
- Data retention policy.
- Xoa du lieu theo yeu cau.

## 13. MVP North Star

Nguoi hoc upload mot tai lieu, he thong tao flashcard va quiz, AI giai thich diem sai, sau do lap lich on tap ca nhan hoa giup nguoi hoc quay lai hoc vao ngay tiep theo.

Neu MVP lam tot vong lap nay, cac module lon hon nhu classroom, parent dashboard, admin, gamification nang cao va collaboration se co nen tang vung de phat trien tiep.

## 15. Product Vision Acceptance Criteria

Product Vision duoc xem la hoan thanh khi:

- Xac dinh ro san pham giai quyet van de gi.
- Xac dinh dung nguoi dung muc tieu.
- Xac dinh gia tri cot loi va diem khac biet.
- Tach duoc MVP scope va post-MVP scope.
- Co metric de do thanh cong.
- Co risk va mitigation.
- Co phase phat trien phu hop voi team 5-10 nguoi.
